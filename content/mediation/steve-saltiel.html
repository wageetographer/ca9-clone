Prior to joining the court in 2019, Steve was an Assistant United States Attorney in the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Northern District of California, serving at times as the Senior Litigation Counsel and Deputy Chief of the Civil Division. Steve is a volunteer panel mediator for the U.S. District Court for the Northern District of California.  Before moving to San Francisco in 1998, Steve was a partner at a small litigation boutique in New York City. Originally from New Jersey, Steve graduated from Benjamin Cardozo School of Law, and George Washington University.
