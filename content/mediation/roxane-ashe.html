Roxane has been a Circuit Mediator for Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals since 1998 and has mediated over two thousand appellate cases. Roxane is also the principal mediator for employment matters brought under the Circuit's Employment Dispute Resolution Plan and serves as a panel mediator for the U.S. District Court for the Northern District of California. Prior to joining the court’s mediation office, Roxane was Supervising Ninth Circuit Motions Attorney, Assistant Circuit Executive for Legal Affairs, and practiced civil litigation at a large firm in St. Louis, Missouri. She earned her J.D. and B.A from the University of Tennessee. Originally from Nashville, Roxane lives by the ocean in Half Moon Bay, California.
