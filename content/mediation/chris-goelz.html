Chris has been a Circuit Mediator for the Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals for 30 years. Before joining the court’s mediation office, he was a Motions Attorney for the Ninth Circuit. Prior to coming to the court Chris was a civil litigator in a small San Francisco firm. Chris has taught negotiation as an Adjunct Professor at the University of Washington School of Law and is an author of a Ninth Circuit practice guide published by the Rutter Group. Chris went to Yale Law School and then clerked for Judge Jonathan Newman of the Oregon Court of Appeal. Chris grew up in Half Moon Bay, California and has an undergraduate degree in mechanical engineering from the University of California, Santa Barbara. Before attending law school, he worked as a production engineer in Silicon Valley.
