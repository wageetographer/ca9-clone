Prior to joining the Ninth Circuit mediation office, Bob mediated federal cases as a Circuit Mediator for the Sixth Circuit Court Appeals and also as a mediator for the U.S. District Court for the Southern District of Ohio. Bob has also mediated hundreds of cases as a private mediator. Before devoting his full-time efforts to mediation, Bob practiced law for 20 years, litigating and trying a wide variety of civil cases. Originally from Ohio, Bob received his J.D. from the University of Cincinnati College of Law and his B.G.S. from the University of Cincinnati.
