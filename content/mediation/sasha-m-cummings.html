Sasha joined the court with 20 years of experience as a partner at a national law firm, trial attorney, and mediator. While in private practice, she served extensively as a Judge Pro Tem, Settlement Conference Officer, and Mandatory Settlement Conference Panelist. She is an Adjunct Professor at USF School of Law and served on the UC Hastings Foundation Board of Trustees. Raised in Northern California’s Wine Country, Sasha earned her J.D. from UC Hastings Law and her B.A. from Wesleyan University in Connecticut.
